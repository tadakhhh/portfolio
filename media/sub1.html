Original Work

SLAM DUNK is a basketball manga by Takehiko Inoue. It was serialized in Weekly Shonen Jump (Shueisha) from issue 42 in 1990 to issue 27 in 1996, and sold over 170 million copies in Japan. There were 31 books in all, and 24 were re-released as "complete editions".

After entering Kamikita High School (Shohoku High School), Hanamichi Sakuragi, a delinquent boy who was scabbed by 50 women during his three years in junior high school, was encouraged to join the basketball club by Haruko Akagi, the younger sister of the basketball club Hanamichi, a beginner, falls in love with at first sight, and through practices and games, he gradually discovers the joy of basketball, and while developing her talent, he aims to win the national championship.
