    This is a movie version of the Interhigh Round of 32 match against Sanno, the final boss of the original, that was not covered in the TV animation, and that many fans wanted. It is similar to the original in its broad outlines, but stands out for its use of directing and storytelling that are different in detail.

    As a representative example, Ryota Miyagi, who did not receive much attention in the original Sanwangden, was given the role of the protagonist, and using his past history as a framework, a directing was added to show all of the Buksan members' pasts in flashbacks.
